# Cisco Newsletter Automation
## Weekly Workflow That Saves 3-4 Hours

---

## The Big Picture

**Before:** Spend 3-4 hours every week researching and writing
**After:** Spend 10 minutes reviewing a ready-made draft

---

## How It Works (Step by Step)

### 🕕 SUNDAY 6:00 PM - Automation Starts

**1. ⏰ TRIGGER (Cron Job)**
- A timer wakes up the system
- Like an alarm clock for your computer
- Works automatically every week

**2. 🔍 TAVILY SEARCH**
- Searches the internet for latest Cisco news
- Finds stories about Security, Networking, and AI
- Like having a research assistant who never sleeps

**3. ✍️ CUPID WRITES**
- AI creates 2-3 executive summaries
- Each summary is 10-15 sentences
- Written in sales-friendly language
- Like having a technical writer on staff

**4. 💾 SAVE TO NOTION**
- Stores the newsletter in an organized database
- Easy to find later
- Like a filing cabinet that never loses anything

**5. 📱 SLACK NOTIFICATION**
- Sends you a message: "Draft is ready!"
- Instant alert on your phone/computer
- Like a secretary tapping you on the shoulder

---

### 🕘 MONDAY 8:00 AM - You Take Over

**6. 👤 YOU REVIEW**
- Read the draft (takes 10 minutes)
- Make minor edits if needed
- Approve when ready

**7. 📧 DISTRIBUTE**
- Send to Distribution Sales Team
- You're in control of final delivery
