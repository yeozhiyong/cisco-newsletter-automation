How It Works

1
Sunday 6:00 PM — Trigger
Automation wakes up and begins the weekly newsletter process.
2
Sunday 6:05 PM — Research
Tavily searches for latest Cisco news across Security, Networking, and AI.
3
Sunday 6:10 PM — Writing
Cupid AI drafts 2-3 executive summaries with 10-15 sentences each.
4
Sunday 6:15 PM — Save & Notify
Newsletter saved to Notion, Slack notification sent to you.
5
Monday 8:00 AM — Review
You review the draft and make minor edits if needed (~10 minutes).
6
Monday 9:00 AM — Distribute
Send the final newsletter to Distribution Sales Team.

Automation Tools

Tavily
Finds latest Cisco news automatically from multiple sources.
Cupid (AI)
Writes executive summaries that understand Cisco context.
Notion
Keeps all newsletters organized and searchable.
Slack
Instant notifications when draft is ready.
Cron Job
Runs every Sunday at 6 PM automatically.
